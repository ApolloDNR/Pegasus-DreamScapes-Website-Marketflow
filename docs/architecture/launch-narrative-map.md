# Launch Narrative Map

Status: working launch map for the Pegasus Dreamscapes public website.
Updated: 2026-06-03.

The site must make Pegasus feel like a premium real estate operating company:
specific, disciplined, safe to work with, and serious enough for professionals.
Every route below has one primary job. If a section does not support the job,
trust proof, conversion, navigation, or compliance boundary, it should be cut.

## Home `/`

Audience: first-time visitors, card recipients, referral partners, sellers,
agents, operators, and investors.

Job: establish Pegasus as The Deal Architect and route the visitor quickly.

Primary CTA: `Submit a Property` -> `/submit`.

Trust proof: four departments, review memo, outcome lanes, Dreamscaper Standard.

Visual purpose: cinematic property hero plus operating diagrams that show
review, structure, and lane routing.

Compliance boundary: every property gets a path; not every property gets an
offer. No valuation, no appraisal, no guaranteed outcome.

## Deal Architecture `/deal-architecture`

Audience: anyone asking what Pegasus actually does.

Job: explain the operating method: read the situation, structure the path,
choose the participation lane, or pass.

Primary CTA: `Submit a Property` -> `/submit`.

Trust proof: situation-first principles, path stack, outcome set, compliance
note.

Visual purpose: architectural/property-to-path diagram, not decoration.

Compliance boundary: informational and strategic only; not an offer, appraisal,
ARV opinion, rehab budget, legal/tax advice, securities offering, or guarantee.

## Submit `/submit`

Audience: owners, agents, wholesalers, operators, referral partners, and anyone
with a real property situation.

Job: capture useful property data and send it into the Pegasus HQ intake bridge.

Primary CTA: `Start the Intake`.

Trust proof: review sequence, consent language, HQ receipt, human review.

Visual purpose: intake packet diagram showing property facts -> review -> route.

Compliance boundary: submission is preliminary and does not create an offer,
agency relationship, valuation, financing commitment, or guaranteed result.

## Connect `/connect`

Audience: QR scans, card recipients, introductions, and in-person conversations.

Job: route the visitor to the correct door in one screen.

Primary CTA: route cards to `/submit`, `/work-with-apollo`, `/development`,
`/capital`, and `/vendor-network`.

Trust proof: direct route logic and minimum useful context.

Visual purpose: card/QR routing map.

Compliance boundary: the card is not the pitch; it is the router. The next lane
depends on review.

## Strategy Lab `/strategy-lab`

Audience: people who want a preliminary property read before submitting.

Job: let users test a property through Pegasus logic, then submit for human
review.

Primary CTA: `Submit to Pegasus` / human Strategy Review.

Trust proof: lane scoring, risk register, scenario inputs, Peggy explain/stress
support, preliminary boundaries.

Visual purpose: premium analysis instrument, not a calculator wall.

Compliance boundary: self-serve read is directional; not an offer, appraisal,
valuation, legal/tax/securities advice, or guaranteed result.

## MarketFlow `/marketflow`

Audience: deal sources, buyers, operators, vendors, and capital relationships.

Job: explain the private reviewed-opportunity network without presenting a
public marketplace.

Primary CTA: `Request Access` -> `/marketflow/access`.

Trust proof: review-before-network rule, role-based access, buy-box filters.

Visual purpose: network map showing HQ review before distribution.

Compliance boundary: private beta, relationship-based access only; not a public
investment marketplace or securities solicitation.

## Work With Apollo `/work-with-apollo`

Audience: sellers, buyers, and investors who may need licensed representation.

Job: separate Apollo's licensed Keller Williams lane from Pegasus operating work.

Primary CTA: `/submit?intent=listing` or contact/listing review.

Trust proof: DRE number, KW East Bay, Equal Housing, lane separation.

Visual purpose: side-by-side Pegasus/KW separation diagram.

Compliance boundary: licensed real estate services are provided by Apollo
through Keller Williams Realty East Bay; Pegasus Dreamscapes Corp is separate.

## Development `/development`

Audience: owners, partners, and operators with ADU, rehab, infill, or value-add
potential.

Job: show development as disciplined value creation, not speculative ambition.

Primary CTA: `/submit?intent=adu`.

Trust proof: mandate, phase ladder, capital/systems/standard support.

Visual purpose: development phase rail and project-control diagrams.

Compliance boundary: future phases are trajectory, not current capability
claims. Work is subject to scope, permits, contracts, licensing, insurance, and
human review.

## Capital `/capital`

Audience: private capital and partnership relationships.

Job: invite private conversations while reducing public-offering risk.

Primary CTA: private introduction/contact route.

Trust proof: relationship-first process, documentation-first posture, explicit
non-offering language.

Visual purpose: private capital ledger/lock diagram.

Compliance boundary: not a securities offering, public fund, public investment
solicitation, return promise, or capital commitment.

## Peggy AI `/peggy-ai`

Audience: visitors who do not know how to explain the situation or where to go.

Job: position Peggy as guided intake and routing support.

Primary CTA: `Submit a Property` or guided conversation.

Trust proof: what Peggy captures, what Peggy can do, what Peggy cannot decide.

Visual purpose: chat/phone/form -> ask/sort/route intake map.

Compliance boundary: Peggy cannot make offers, approve deals, guarantee
outcomes, replace licensed/professional judgment, or release execution
decisions.

## Contact `/contact`

Audience: people who do not fit a more specific door.

Job: route general inquiries without breaking the canonical intake model.

Primary CTA: seller/property -> `/submit?intent=property`, partner -> `/capital`,
everything else -> form.

Trust proof: direct contact path and plain-language message form.

Visual purpose: minimal; contact should not compete with the core conversion
doors.

Compliance boundary: contact does not create agency, capital, investment, or
execution relationship.
