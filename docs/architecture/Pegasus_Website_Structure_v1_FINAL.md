Pegasus DreamScapes is a **deal architecture and development company** that turns complex, distressed, and value-add properties into structured opportunities.

We assess the property, the situation, the people, and the numbers, then route the opportunity to the best outcome:

- Acquire
- Develop
- Renovate
- List
- Represent
- Wholesale
- JV
- Fund
- Refer
- Pass

**Public promise:** Complex property. Structured opportunity.

**Doctrine line:** The magic is in the fluidity: the service, the path, and the outcome.

- Give each core audience a clean door.

3. For Wholesalers & Agents
4. For Investors & Operators
5. Capital & Partnerships

### Development Page

Development replaces a separate top-nav Projects page. Projects live inside Development as proof.

Structure:

1. **Development Overview** — Development is the spine. Strategy is how we choose the path.
2. **ADU & Forced-Value Strategy** — California ADU lane and signature niche.
3. **Rehabs & Repositioning** — flip, wholetail, BRRRR, rental repositioning.
4. **JV / Co-Development** — owners or partners with equity, land, money, or opportunity.
5. **Projects & Case Studies** — Nelson Dr first, future projects added.

Compliance boundary:

Hold all language about Pegasus DreamScapes Developments LLC at **planned** / **subject to final entity, licensing, insurance, and contract review** until formation is verified.

### Strategy Lab Page

Self-serve interactive workspace, not a calculator wall.

1. Enter property

- Wholetail

- Rental Hold
- ADU + Development
- Ground-Up
- Wholesale
- JV
- Listing or Operator Referral

Required disclaimer:

Strategy Lab gives a preliminary read, not a final offer, appraisal, ARV, rehab budget, or guaranteed result.

### Peggy AI Page

Lives under More.

Positioning:

**Peggy is Pegasus's AI Strategy Assistant. One intelligence, multiple surfaces.**

Sections:

1. What Peggy is — not a chatbot, an assistant.
2. Where Peggy works — website, phone, Pegasus HQ, across the ecosystem.
3. What Peggy does — guide intake, ask clarifying questions, explain high-level strategies, summarize property situations, recommend a route, prepare Strategy Snapshot drafts, identify missing information.
4. What Peggy does not do — no offers, no values quoted, no profit guarantees, no deal approvals, no legal/tax/securities/permit advice, no movement of money.
5. Sample transcript.
6. CTA: Start a conversation.

Peggy also lives as a persistent floating widget across all pages. Design: subtle copper-accent pill, not a giant chat bubble.

Phone routing through Peggy is a future capability requiring Phil and legal review before deployment. It is not a v1 launch item.
