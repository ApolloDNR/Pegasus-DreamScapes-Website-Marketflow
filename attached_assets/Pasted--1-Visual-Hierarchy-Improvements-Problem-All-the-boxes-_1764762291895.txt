✅ 1. Visual Hierarchy Improvements
🔸 Problem:

All the boxes (Weekly Pulse, Open Opportunities, Saved Matches, Active Investments, Notifications) look similar in size and weight.

⭐ Solution:

Make the dashboard feel like a command center by:

Increasing contrast between top KPIs and lower sections

Highlighting important metrics with stronger color tokens (green for gains, red for losses, gold for Pegasus brand)

Using mini icons consistently (ARROW ↑, DEAL icon, MONEY icon)

Result:

Your dashboard feels more “alive,” like a trading platform or SaaS backend.

Why:

It makes the dashboard feel like a productivity hub, not just a reporting screen.
