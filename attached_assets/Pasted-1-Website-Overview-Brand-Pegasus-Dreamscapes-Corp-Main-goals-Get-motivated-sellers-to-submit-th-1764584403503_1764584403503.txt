What you do:

Provide a clear offer strategy (cash offer or listing strategy in future)

Service 2 – Buy & Hold / Rentals

Who it’s for: investors who want cash-flowing rentals.

What you do:

Who it’s for: owners who already have a property.

What you do:

Quick blurb about offering 1:1 deal review or project consultation.

5. How You Can Use This Blueprint

In Replit:
Use this as your roadmap. Start with a single-page site (Home + sections for About, Services, Sell, Invest, Projects, Contact) and later break into separate routes/pages.

For AI / code helpers:
You can literally paste chunks of this into Replit AI or another assistant and say:

“Build me a responsive landing page section in React/HTML that matches this text and uses a dark theme with bronze/orange accents.”

For copy:
You already have ~80–90% of the wording above. You’ll only need to:

Insert your real numbers,

Swap or add project names,

Adjust tone where you want it more “Paolo / Pegasus” sounding.
