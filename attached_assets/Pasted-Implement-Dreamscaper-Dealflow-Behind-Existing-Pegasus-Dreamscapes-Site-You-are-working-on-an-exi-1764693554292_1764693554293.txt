DREAMSCAPER – vetted operator / flipper / project lead

WHOLESALER – posts off-market deals

INVESTOR – deploys capital or partners as LP/GP

BUYER – buys wholesale or renovated properties

On signup, ask:

“What best describes you?”
[Dreamscaper] [Wholesaler] [Investor] [Buyer]

Users can later add more roles in Settings.

7.3 Negotiation Flow

Dreamscaper can Accept, Decline, or Counter each offer.

Accept → creates CommittedInvestment, updates progress bar.

Counter → allows editing amount/terms, sets status to COUNTERED.

Both sides see a negotiation thread per offer—like chat, but with term snapshots.

Investor Office shows “My Offers & Negotiations” with statuses.
